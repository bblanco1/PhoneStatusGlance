When you’re out and about, you don’t want to have to keep taking your phone out of your pocket to check if you’ve finally got a decent cellular signal. And you don’t want to have to pull your iPad out of your bag to see if your battery needs topping up at the last charging point at the airport before boarding.

With Phone Status Glance, just raise your wrist and swipe for a quick update on what’s going on with your device. Simple, straightforward information in an easy to use package.
